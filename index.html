Fourier Number Embedding (FNE)
directly maps numbers into their Fourier representations, bypassing the tokenization step entirely
with Better Efficiency and Accuracy.

Why Design Like This?

FNE leverages Fourier-based number embeddings to efficiently encode numerical information directly into a continuous representation. By avoiding tokenization, FNE achieves:

  • More precise number representation
  • Faster computation due to fewer model parameters
  • Better generalization across different numerical values
